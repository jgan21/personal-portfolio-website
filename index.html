About.

Hi, I'm Jia, a full-stack software engineer with a background in classical music. Before diving into the world of tech, I was fully immersed in the world of violin, playing professionally at orchestras and string quartets all over the globe.

During the pandemic, like many others, I found myself reflecting on my passions and exploring new avenues, and it was during this time that I noticed a fascinating trend emerging: many musicians were making a transition into the tech industry, particularly software engineering. This piqued my curiosity and led me down a path of exploration. What drew me in was the striking parallel between music and coding—both demand a keen eye for detail, a knack for recognizing patterns, and a deep appreciation for creativity. Just as a musician crafts melodies, a software engineer crafts elegant solutions to complex problems.

So, I took the leap. I immersed myself in coding tutorials, online courses, and collaborative projects. With each line of code, I discovered a new way to express myself and make a tangible impact on the world.

Today, as a software engineer, I bring the same passion and dedication from my musical background into my work. I thrive on the challenge of building products that not only meet technical standards but also enrich people's lives.
